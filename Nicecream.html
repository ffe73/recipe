Coconut-Raspberries Nicecream

Info

Difficulty
Duration
5:00 H
This serves
5 people

Ingredients

1
can coconut milk
1
package of vanilla sugar
1
bowl of raspberries
Some
honey to sweeten

Preparation

  1. Foam coconut milk with bourbon vanilla sugar in a blender and sweeten it with honey.
  2. Bring the raspberries to boil with a little water and honey and mash them with a fork.
  3. Put the coconut milk into molds and place them in the freezer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Take it out and fill up with the raspberry puree.
  5. Freeze it for 30-60 minutes and then stick the handle or spoon in.
  6. Then place it in the freezer for at least four hours until the ice has set.
