Preparation

  1. Roast the almonds in a pan without fat. Then set aside. Prepare the pasta in plenty of salted water according to the instructions on the packet.
  2. In the meantime, clean the peppers, zucchini and mushrooms, wash as necessary and cut into bite-sized pieces. Wash tomatoes, pat dry and cut in half. Peel and halve the onion and cut into large pieces. Peel garlic and chop finely. Wash oregano, shake dry, pull off the leaves and cut finely. Wash lamb's lettuce, shake dry and clean.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the onion in it. Add the bell pepper, zucchini, mushrooms, garlic and oregano and fry. Season with salt and pepper. Deglaze everything with vinegar and tomato juice and bring to the boil once. Drain the pasta and add to the pan. Mix in the tomatoes.
  4. Arrange the pasta salad in a bowl and garnish with almonds and lamb's lettuce.
